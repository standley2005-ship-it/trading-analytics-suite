# Risk Management Notes

## Core Rules

- Define risk before entry.
- Avoid taking trades without a stop-loss plan.
- Keep trade size small enough that one loss does not damage the account.
- Track wins, losses, setup quality, and emotional mistakes.

## Position Sizing Concept

```text
risk_amount = account_size * risk_percent
position_size = risk_amount / distance_to_stop
```

## Trade Journal Fields

- Date
- Ticker or pair
- Setup type
- Entry
- Stop
- Target
- Result
- Notes
